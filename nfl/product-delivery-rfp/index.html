Questions to Submit by Jul 14

Grouped by theme — targeted questions to expose scope depth, complexity, and hidden operational overhead.

Scope & Volume6 Questions
+
  1. What is the current backlog size and roadmap horizon for FY27–FY28?
  2. How many concurrent initiatives/epics are in-flight across the 7 pods?
  3. What is current sprint velocity per team and target velocity uplift?
  4. Expected split between new features vs. bug fixes vs. tech debt?
  5. Are NFL RedZone, NFL+ Premium, and NFL Network programming in scope?
  6. Expected partner headcount ballpark — augmentation or replacement?
Team & Engagement Model5 Questions
+
  1. Will partner own end-to-end pods or embed individuals across NFL-led pods?
  2. Expected split between NFL FTE and partner in each pod?
  3. Reporting lines for partner leads (Delivery Lead, Tech Lead)?
  4. Required Pacific Time overlap window (4 hrs, 6 hrs, full workday)?
  5. Game-day on-site: how many resources, how many weekends, billable travel?
Technology & Architecture5 Questions
+
  1. Availability of architecture diagram / system map (NDA-gated)?
  2. Backend services, CMS, streaming stack, CDN — Node.js/BFF layers in scope?
  3. Design system maturity (Figma tokens, cross-platform parity)?
  4. Preferred AI/ML platforms (SageMaker, Vertex, in-house)?
  5. Pre-approved AI coding tools (Copilot, Cursor, Claude Code)?
Governance, Quality & SLAs4 Questions
+
  1. Quantitative quality gates today (defect escape, uptime %, Core Web Vitals)?
  2. How is "zero-tolerance downtime" contractually defined — service credits?
  3. Incident response expectation (24/7 on-call, MTTR targets)?
  4. KPIs tied to NFL+ subscriber growth that partner will be measured on?
Transition, Continuity & Risk4 Questions
+
  1. Are there incumbent partners? Knowledge-transfer window?
  2. Expected ramp curve from signing (Dec 2026) to full productivity (Apr 2027)?
  3. Acceptable delivery geographies (US-only, nearshore, offshore)?
  4. Subcontracting restrictions to nearshore/offshore centers?
Commercial4 Questions
+
  1. Preferred pricing model (T&M, retainer, outcome-based, hybrid)?
  2. Annual rate escalations — tied to what index?
  3. Volume commitments or minimum guarantees?
  4. Shared-risk/gain-share tied to NFL+ subscriber milestones?
Legal & Compliance3 Questions
+
  1. Draft MSA / standard terms — especially IP warranty for AI-assisted output?
  2. Background-check standard (NFL-defined or provider-defined)?
  3. Preferred cyber-insurance minimum coverage?
