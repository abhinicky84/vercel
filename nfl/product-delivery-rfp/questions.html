Category A — Scope & Volume

Questions relating to backlog size, velocity, and coverage boundaries to right-size our proposed engagement.
A1.1#8 Project Overview
What is the current backlog size (story points or initiative count) and roadmap horizon for FY27–FY28 across the 7 pods (Backend, CTV, DTC, Identity, Mobile, Video, Web)?
Rationale: To right-size proposed pod structure and forecast capacity requirements.
A1.2#10.1 Current Needs
How many concurrent initiatives/epics are typically in-flight simultaneously across all pods, and what is the average duration of an epic?
Rationale: To model delivery cadence and validate 2-week sprint fit vs. longer initiative planning cycles.
A1.3#10.1 Current Needs
What is the current sprint velocity (average story points/sprint) per pod, and what target velocity uplift is expected from partner engagement?
Rationale: To establish baseline metrics and set realistic uplift commitments in our proposal.
A1.4#10.1 Current Needs
Is there a target split between new feature development, bug fixes, and technical debt (e.g., 60/20/20)? How is technical debt currently prioritized?
Rationale: To align capacity allocation and understand tech debt burden inherited on Day 1.
A1.5#7 Company Description
Are NFL RedZone, NFL+ Premium features, and NFL Network programming apps included in this scope, or handled by separate teams/partners?
Rationale: To confirm boundaries and avoid scope ambiguity in pricing.
A1.6#8 Project Overview
What is the anticipated total partner headcount ballpark (single pod, multiple pods, dozens of resources)? Is this augmentation of the current ~74-person team or partial replacement?
Rationale: To right-size our response and understand competitive dynamics.

Category B — Team & Engagement Model

Questions about operating structure, reporting lines, and time-zone requirements.
B2.1#11 Current State Context
Will the selected partner own end-to-end product pods, or embed individuals within existing NFL-led pods? Is a hybrid model acceptable?
Rationale: To design the appropriate operating model and role definitions.
B2.2#11 Current State Context
What is the expected ratio of NFL FTE to partner resources within each pod (e.g., 50/50, 30/70)?
Rationale: To structure blended teams and clarify accountability boundaries.
B2.3#17.6 Governance
Will partner-provided leads (Delivery Lead, Tech Lead) report to NFL Product Owners/Engineering Managers, or operate with functional autonomy?
Rationale: To align governance model and reporting structures.

Category C — Technology & Architecture

Questions to understand the current technical estate and integration surface.
C3.1#11 Current State Context
Is there an architecture diagram or system topology map available (under NDA) showing CTV/DTC/Video/Identity service relationships?
Rationale: To assess technical complexity and identify integration risks.
C3.2#16.6 Technology Stack
Which backend services, CMS, video streaming stack, and CDN do the frontend applications interface with? Are Node.js/BFF (Backend-for-Frontend) layers part of the frontend scope?
Rationale: To confirm true scope boundaries beyond the "frontend" React/RN stack.
C3.3#10.1 Current Needs
What is the current maturity of the design system? Are Figma tokens implemented, is there a cross-platform component library, and what parity exists between web/mobile/CTV?
Rationale: To understand design system evolution work vs. maintenance work.
C3.4#10.2 Future Needs
Which AI/ML platforms and infrastructure are currently used or preferred (AWS SageMaker, Vertex AI, in-house)? Are there existing personalization/recommendation engines the partner would inherit?
Rationale: To align AI/ML capability delivery and avoid rebuilding existing infrastructure.
C3.5#16.12 AI Governance
Which AI coding tools are pre-approved by NFL (e.g., GitHub Copilot Business, Cursor, Claude Code, Amazon Q)? What is the approval process for proposing new tools?
Rationale: To confirm AI toolchain readiness and any onboarding overhead.
